HOW TO USE THIS PLAYBOOK

The intended audience of this playbook will be the Chief Information Officer (CIO), Chief Technology Officer (CTO), Directors, Department Chiefs, and IT Managers as a primer to educate the reader about digital transformation concepts and the considerations and trade space involved with transforming to modern IT environments.

This primer outlines how the major building blocks described in the subsequent chapters of this playbook intersect to achieve the common goal of modernizing IT infrastructure, sharing and analyzing data and reengineering business processes and culture to bring the enterprise into a stage that will be agile enough to adopt and deploy technologies at a pace that is consistent with the rate of change of today’s technological advances. With this in mind, the goal of this Digital Transformation Playbook will be to lower the barrier to entry for the less technical consumers of technology and level the playing field. Each building block may be leveraged by any organization as they see fit.

This playbook is divided into 4 logical sections:

Chapter 1

Explains our viewpoint of the Digital Transformation journey to explain the meaning of digital transformation, the compelling need to transform, the overarching considerations during transformation, and relevance to the Intelligence Community.

Chapter 2

Defines our portfolio based approach to digital transformation known as our Enterprise Integration framework which serves to help organizations achieve an open, agile stage of IT maturity by addressing the shift toward “on-demand” solutions and maximizing the innovations of shared services, platform standardization & optimization, application rationalization, enterprise cloud capabilities, mobile device integration, and big data analytics.

Chapter 3

Explains digital transformation in context of the IC’s cloud reference implementation and the value proposition enabled by a cloud services model.

CHAPTERS 4,5, AND 6

Provides a three-tiered approach for considering people, process, and technology and outlines the best practices, roadmaps, approaches, and techniques that organizations can leverage to help guide critical decisions during the journey to a successful Digital Enterprise.
